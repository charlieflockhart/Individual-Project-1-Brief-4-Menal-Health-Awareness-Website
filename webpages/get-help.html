Helpful Resources

Childline is a free, confidential helpline in the UK for children and young people under 19 who need someone to talk to about any issue or problem.

The service is free to call at 0800 1111 and is also available online through chats or email.

Visit their website at Childline for more information.

The Mix is a UK-based support service aimed at helping young people aged 13-25 navigate life's challenges.

Support Available:

  • Helpline: Call 0808 808 4994 for free.
  • Online Chat: Speak directly to trained supporters via their website.
  • Email:Receive advice through email correspondence.

Visit their website at The Mix for more information.

CALM (Campaign Against Living Miserably) is a UK-based charity focused on preventing suicide and supporting individuals struggling with their mental health, particularly men, who are at higher risk of suicide.

Helpline and Webchat: Open 365 days a year, from 5 PM to midnight. Call 0800 58 58 58 or access the webchat on their website.

Visit their website at CALM for more information.

SANEline is a UK-based mental health helpline provided by the charity SANE, which focuses on improving the quality of life for people affected by mental illness, including those experiencing it directly, as well as their families, friends, and caregivers.

SANEline operates 365 days a year, from 4:30 PM - 10:30 PM. The number is 0300 304 7000.

Visit their website at SANEline for more information.
